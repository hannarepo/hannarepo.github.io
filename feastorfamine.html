    • Project

    Feast Or Famine is a small third person game where the player tries to keep the villagers alive and working the field by gathering food from the forest by killing enemies and fishing.

    The project is built with Unity 6 Universal Render Pipeline (URP).

    In this project my main responsibilities were:

  • Leading code direction and structure and keeping track of the progress of the project
  • Implementing player mechanics
  • Implementing camera controls
  • Implementing enemy behaviour and functionality
  • Implementing villager wandering

    • Key features

  • Melee combat
  • Resource management
  • Enemies with basic AI
  • Fishing minigame

    • Player and enemies

    I implemented the player mechanics with a hierarchical state machine. The player can walk, run, swipe and thrust attack and dodge roll.

    There are two types of enemies, a wild boar and a large plant. The boar has a set path with waypoints that it will patrol on and will detect player when they are within the boar's aggro range and vision cone. The boar has a charge attack where it will charge towards the player and is briefly exhausted after.

    The plant enemy cannot move other than rotate slowly to face the player. It has two types of attacks, a swipe attack and an overhead between which it will choose based on player proximity and direction. The enemies are also implemented with a hierarchical state machine.
