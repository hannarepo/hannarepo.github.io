    • Project

    Feast Or Famine is a small third-person resource gathering game built in Unity 6 URP for Windows. The player keeps villagers alive and working the fields by gathering food from the forest through combat and fishing, while managing light survival elements.

    In this project, I worked as code lead and was responsible for guiding code structure, tracking programming progress, and implementing several core gameplay systems. My main responsibilities included player mechanics, enemy functionality, villager wandering, camera controls, and level design. My work focused on building the gameplay systems that support combat, exploration, and the game’s core resource-gathering loop.

    Key features

  • Melee combat
  • Resource management
  • Enemies with basic AI
  • Fishing minigame

    • Player and enemies

    I implemented the player character using a hierarchical state machine. The player can walk, run, perform swipe and thrust attacks, and dodge roll, with the state-based structure helping keep the character logic organized and easier to expand.

    I also implemented two enemy types using hierarchical state machines. The wild boar patrols along waypoint routes, detects the player through aggro range and vision cone checks, and uses a charge attack followed by a short exhaustion period.
    The plant enemy remains stationary apart from rotating to face the player, and selects between two attacks based on the player's proximity and direction. In addition, I implemented villager wandering behavior with waypoint routes and camera controls.
